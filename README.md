# Jarurat Care | Mini Healthcare Support

**Jarurat Care** is a single-page healthcare support and volunteer registration portal designed to connect individuals seeking medical assistance with a network of volunteers and professionals. It provides an accessible platform for patients to request help and for volunteers to easily sign up and contribute to their communities.

## 💻 Tech Stack
This application is designed to be lightweight, easy to deploy, and maintainable without needing heavy frameworks.

*   **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Vanilla), JavaScript (Vanilla/ES6+)
*   **Design & Styling:** Custom CSS featuring dynamic gradients, glassmorphism UI elements, and responsive layouts.
*   **Icons:** FontAwesome (via CDN).
*   **Local Development Server:** Python (`http.server`) for simply and dependably serving the application locally without setup overhead.

## 🤖 AI Idea (Chatbot Assistant)
The project integrates a **Dynamic AI Chatbot Concept ("Jarurat Care AI")** designed specifically for healthcare triage and support:

*   **Customizable Knowledge Base:** Admins and support staff can use the built-in *Bot Setup* dashboard to add new keywords and responses on the fly. 
*   **Intelligent Routing:** The bot parses user input for specific medical or volunteer-related phrasing. Depending on the inquiry, it can provide immediate answers about insurance, support services, and contact information, or direct users to fill out the registration form.
*   **Emulated AI Parsing:** The current implementation uses a pattern-matching algorithm to provide conversational responses based on predefined rules, demonstrating how a specialized language model could interface with users directly on the site.

## 🤝 NGO Use-Case
Modern Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) and community groups often struggle to manage the influx of patient requests and volunteer sign-ups simultaneously. **Jarurat Care** solves this by providing a unified portal:

*   **Patient Support & Care Routing:** Patients looking for telemedicine, mental health counseling, or local care routing can easily submit targeted requests via the *Support* form. 
*   **Volunteer Registration:** The same form seamlessly serves as an onboarding channel for new volunteers who want to participate in the NGO's healthcare initiatives.
*   **Reduced Administrative Overhead:** By placing an AI assistant at the forefront, the NGO can deflect hundreds of "General Inquiry" texts and calls (e.g., questions about cost, location, and how to get involved), allowing human coordinators to safely focus on critical medical needs and complex cases.
